Blue Plates

Started in 2022 as a college project by three first year B.Tech students, then bloomed as a side project while they were busy in their corporate lives and later flourished as a full time business to provide excellent ambience and quality food along with the customization of menu items as per customer's preference

Food is prepared with the state of the art culinary skills acquired by our experienced chefs in their years of hard work and practice. The garden-fresh ingredients are used in the kitchen, dishes are prepared by passionate chefs and served with warmth of love by our staff.

We have been serving since 2022, and wish to do it for long. We have our branches in Phagwara, Jalandhar and Chandigarh. Blue Plates name was chosen because of the color of the Sky which inspires us to do more because Sky has no limits

Some Healthy Tips

  • Always start with light appetizer/sald
  • Don't hesitate to ask for extra spicy food if you like it but it's not recommended
  • You can fill up on vegetables (but you can prepare it at home too :p)
  • Too Much of everything is not always good, keep this in mind for salt and sugar
  • Ending the meal with something sweet is good, but with lemonade it's always better
